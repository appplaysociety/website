What is PlaySociety

Society sports,
finally organised

PlaySociety is a mobile platform built specifically for residential societies in India. It digitises the entire sports coaching lifecycle — from enrollment and scheduling to attendance tracking and fee management.

Whether you're a parent enrolling your child in badminton, a coach managing multiple batches, or an RWA admin approving coaches — PlaySociety puts everything in your pocket.

🏸 Badminton 🏏 Cricket ⚽ Football 🏊 Swimming 🎾 Tennis 🏋️ Fitness + Any Sport
🏘️

Built for Indian Societies

Designed ground-up for residential society sports programmes — gated communities, apartment complexes, and housing colonies across India. Supports OTP-based login, Indian phone numbers, and INR fee management.

How It Works

Up and running in minutes

No training required. The app is built around how societies actually work.

1

RWA registers society

The RWA admin creates the society profile and lists available sports and facilities.

2

Coach applies

A certified coach applies to coach a sport in the society. RWA reviews and approves.

3

Students enroll

Parents browse available batches and enroll their children. Fees are auto-generated.

4

Everyone stays informed

Sessions, attendance, fees, and performance — all tracked and visible in real time.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

PlaySociety ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use the PlaySociety mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

  • Phone number — used for account creation and OTP-based authentication
  • Name and profile information — provided during registration
  • Usage data — app interactions, session attendance, fee records
  • Device information — device type and OS version for app compatibility

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To authenticate your identity via OTP (powered by Twilio)
  • To provide core app functionality — enrollment, attendance, fee management
  • To send you app notifications about your sessions and fees
  • To improve the PlaySociety platform

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We share data only with:

  • Twilio — for sending OTP SMS messages (phone number only)
  • Google Cloud Platform — for secure data storage and hosting
  • Your society's coach and RWA admin — limited profile and attendance data as required for the sports program

4. Data Security

All data is transmitted over HTTPS and stored securely on Google Cloud Platform. Authentication tokens are stored in your device's encrypted secure storage (Android Keystore / iOS Keychain).

5. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us at hello@playsociety.in.

6. Your Rights

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and data
  • Withdraw consent at any time

7. Children's Privacy

PlaySociety is intended for users 18 and above. Parents may manage accounts on behalf of minor children. We do not knowingly collect data directly from children under 13.

8. Contact

For any privacy-related questions or requests, contact us at: hello@playsociety.in
